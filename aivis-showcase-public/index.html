AiVIS CITE LEDGER AI Visibility System

AiVIS is a CITE LEDGER-based AI visibility system that measures how web content is interpreted, trusted, and cited by AI answer engines such as ChatGPT, Perplexity, and Gemini.

Evidence-based AI visibility and citation scoring for answer engine optimization

AiVIS replaces traditional SEO keyword metrics with AI readability and citability scoring. It evaluates how large language models parse, interpret, and reuse web content in generated answers.

Core system: CITE LEDGER

CITE LEDGER is the internal attribution system powering AiVIS. It maps how AI systems break down content, what external sources reinforce trust, and where a brand appears as a cited reference in AI-generated responses.

This creates a measurable record of AI interpretation behavior across answer engines, forming the basis for visibility scoring and citation tracking.

How AiVIS works

The system operates as a continuous loop: content is scanned, AI interpretation gaps are identified, structured fixes are applied, and visibility changes are measured through re-analysis.

This cycle ensures AI visibility is treated as a measurable system rather than a static SEO outcome.

System identity

AiVIS and CITE LEDGER are a single integrated system. AiVIS is the visibility platform, and CITE LEDGER is the attribution mechanism that tracks how AI systems interpret and cite web content.
