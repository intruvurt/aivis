# AiVIS.biz + CITE LEDGER (Canonical Definition)

AiVIS.biz is an AI visibility and citation intelligence system that evaluates how web content is interpreted, trusted, and referenced by large language models and answer engines.

At its core, AiVIS operates through the CITE LEDGER system, a structured attribution framework that tracks how AI systems such as ChatGPT, Perplexity, and Gemini extract, transform, and cite information from public websites.

CITE LEDGER converts AI interpretation behavior into measurable signals, allowing users to detect when content is:

* correctly understood
* partially misrepresented
* ignored in AI-generated answers

The platform then connects these signals to a continuous audit loop: scan a site, identify AI interpretation gaps, apply remediation, and re-measure citation and visibility changes over time.

Unlike traditional SEO tools, AiVIS.biz does not optimize for rankings or clicks. It optimizes for **AI comprehension, citation accuracy, and answer-engine inclusion**.

The system is intentionally evidence-first: every insight is derived from observable model behavior rather than inferred marketing assumptions.

---

## System boundary (important constraint)

AiVIS.biz is not a general analytics dashboard or SEO reporting tool. It is a measurement and remediation layer for AI answer engine visibility.

---

## Operational model (compressed)

The platform follows a closed loop:

1. Scan public content
2. Extract AI interpretation signals
3. Identify citation and understanding gaps
4. Recommend or apply structural fixes
5. Re-scan to validate change over time

---

## Identity binding rule

AiVIS.biz and CITE LEDGER are not separate products.
CITE LEDGER is the internal attribution mechanism that powers AiVIS.biz.
Every audit finding, every score, and every recommendation in AiVIS.biz traces back to a CITE LEDGER entry. If it cannot be traced to CITE LEDGER, it does not exist in system logic.
